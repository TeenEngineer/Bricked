# Bricked
Hi! I made this app because I was bored and also that I was really addicted to social media on my phone, and I needed an app like Brick. But I needed a physical Brick that was just a simple NFC tag in a 3D printed enclosure, and I live in a country far away from the US. So I couldn't really buy it(also because of the high price). So I decided to make an alternative that has similar blocking methods with customizable NFC tags(and I mean it!). You can even use your bank card to unlock or lock your phone! Enjoy the app! (**The app itself is in Releases!**)
